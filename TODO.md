## üí° Usage Tips & Reminders

**Daily:**
- Log runs immediately after completing them
- Don't wait - details fade fast!

**Weekly:**
- Complete Sunday reflection every week
- Review upcoming week's plan
- Check overall progress

**Throughout Training:**
- Screenshot important milestones as backup
- Be honest about missed runs (log them!)
- Trust the process - consistency beats perfection

**The 2-Day Rule:**
- Can skip 1 scheduled run (life happens)
- CANNOT skip 2 consecutive scheduled runs
- If you miss 2, that's choosing to quit (be honest!)
